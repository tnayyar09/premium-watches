✦ Our Story

Crafting Timeless Moments Since 2010

Luxe Watches was founded with a singular passion — to bring the world's finest timepieces to discerning collectors and enthusiasts in India. What started as a small boutique in Mumbai has grown into one of the most trusted names in luxury watch retail.

Our curated collection spans from iconic Swiss luxury brands to innovative smartwatches, ensuring every wrist finds its perfect companion. Each watch in our inventory undergoes rigorous authentication, so you can buy with complete confidence.
